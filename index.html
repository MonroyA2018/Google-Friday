Achievement Recorder

(Remember that this is just a prototype)

Description

This Achievement Recorder initially help RFO's to record all of the cadets awards fro them to know what they have earned so that they don't wear unauthorized awards.
Is also for them to record requirements for other awards that cadet can get.

Ribbons

This are the most common of awards, cadets earn them by acumulating merits, hours of work, being part of a team, etc.

Tabs

The tabs are earnd by being part of an special event in an specific unit, or by winning individual competitions.

Cords

This are awarded to the cadets who are part of an specific unit, or in case of being individual, by acomplishing specific tasks or by being the head of a unit.

National awards and Medals

This are the hardest awards to get. They are usually awarded for outstanding performance in an specific competition or by being the best or most hardworking cadet in a unit, they take a lot of effort, dedication, and working hours.

Awards Record

These are some exapmles of how
the awards are going to be recorded and the reason of the recorded award.

  • Ribbons

    (Easiest to get)
  • Leader of the Month ribbon
    For outsanding performance in the Leader of the month board, resulting winner.
  • Squad Leader Commendation ribbon
    For outstanding service as a squad leader in Bravo company.
  • Silver Rifles ribbon
    For performing with the drill team at a nationl competition.

  • Cords

    (Not very common)
  • SR Exhibition cord
    For being part of the exhibition drill team.
  • Color Guard Primary cord
    For being part of the color guard for 1 full year.
  • SR Regulation cord
    For being part of the Regulation team and perform at a competition.
  • Honor Comitee
    For following the honor code and carrying it with pride and being part of HC.

  • Medals

    (Hard to get)
  • Individual Drill medal
    Awarded to a new an old boy cadet that is considered the best drill cadet.
  • Squad Drill medal
    Awarded to the squad which is considered the best drill squad as a result of drill comps.
  • Bravo Company Best New Boy medal
    Awarded to the new cadet that is considered the best cadet in Bravo company.

  • Tabs

    (Not very common)
  • Color Guard tab
    Awarded to all cadets that have been part of the color guard for 3 months.
  • Drill Team tab
    Awarded to the cadets that are part of the drill team and have performed with the team.
  • Leader of the Month tab
    Awarded to the cadet that is the best leader of the month determined by an officers board.

  • National Awards

    (The hardest to get)
  • Military Excellence award
    Awarded to an underclass-men cadet that is considered the most shrap cadet in the company.
  • Governors Award
    Awarded to the Junior cadet from the state of Wisconsin that is considered the best cadet.
  • The President's Award
    Awarded to the cadet that is considered the best cadet in the corp of cadets.
